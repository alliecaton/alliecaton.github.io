Hello!!

Check out a few of my favorite projects below!

Keenville

Keenville is a Live Operations game-based assessment application made up of 31 minigames tied to different education standards. Tens of thousands of public school learners in Georgia immerse themselves in the adorable world of Keenville, providing teachers valuable information on their learning progress accessible through a Teacher Dashboard.

Role: Co-producer

The Family Dinner Project

The Family Dinner Project is a Wordpress website that focuses on bringing families together during dinnertime with recipes, activities, and conversation topics. This is a live operations project that is currently and continuously maintained.

Role: Producer

The Word Collector Film
